# Daily Reflection Tree - Design Explanation

## Approach
This reflection system is designed to guide users through structured thinking across three psychological axes:
- Locus of control (agency vs externalization)
- Contribution vs entitlement
- Radius of concern (self vs others)

## Design Philosophy
The goal was not to evaluate the user, but to help them observe their own patterns.

Questions were designed to:
- Feel natural after a workday
- Offer realistic options (not ideal answers)
- Encourage honest self-selection

## Structure
The flow moves progressively:
1. Understanding reactions (agency)
2. Examining contribution
3. Expanding awareness toward others

Each axis builds on the previous one.

## Determinism
All interactions are fully deterministic:
- Fixed options only
- Predefined transitions
- No runtime AI usage

## Improvements
With more time:
- Add deeper branching
- Personalize summaries using signal tracking
- Expand edge-case handling
